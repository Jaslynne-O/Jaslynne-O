    Responsive website that tells an adventurous story and tracks player decisions with multiple endings. Implemented a contact form with validation and email integration.

    • including layout design, responsive UI, and user experience considerations. Implementing interactive features such as timed decisions, a back button to reverse choices,
    • progress tracking, and customizable settings (dark mode, sound controls, text speed, difficulty levels) enhanced understanding of dynamic content management and state handling in web applications.
